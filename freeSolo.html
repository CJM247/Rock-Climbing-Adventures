What is Free Solo Rock Climbing?

Free solo climbing is a form of rock climbing where the climber ascends without any ropes, harnesses, or protective gear. This style relies entirely on the climber's skill, strength, and mental focus, making it one of the most challenging and daring forms of climbing.
